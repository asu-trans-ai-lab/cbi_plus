How to read the results (teaching points): (1) Masking geometry decides the AI winner — with random single cells, interpolation is unbeatable (0.53 km/h); with realistic 2-h dark blocks its congested-cell error grows 7× (11.0 km/h) and completion families close in (matrix completion 12.5). (2) Training span matters — on a 7-day sample the completion/tensor families are rank-starved; rerun on the full month before drawing conclusions. (3) No queue shape wins everywhere — trapezoid takes oversaturated episodes (301/450), quadratic the mild ones (122). (4) PINN is registered and SKIPPED unless torch is present — coupling via the corridor contract is the staged next step (ENGINES.md).
